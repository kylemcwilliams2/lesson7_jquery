Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable order
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder

Instructions

Preheat Waffle Iron.

Beat eggs in large bowl with hand beater until fluffy.

beat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt, and vanilla until smooth.

Spray preheated waffle iron with nonstick cooking spray. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron.
